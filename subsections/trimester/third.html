Healthy Tips in the Third Trimester (27–40 Weeks)

The third trimester focuses on comfort, preparation, and readiness for birth. As your baby continues to grow, your body works harder to support both of you.

Nutrition and Hydration

  • Eat small, frequent meals to reduce heartburn
  • Maintain a balanced diet with lean protein, fiber, calcium, iron, and healthy fats to prevent constipation
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and support circulation
  • Continue taking prenatal vitamins as advised

Calorie note: Most women need about 450 extra calories per day during this stage to support baby growth.

Rest and Comfort

  • Sleep on your left side to improve blood flow
  • Use pillows to support your back and legs
  • Elevate your feet to reduce swelling
  • Take regular rest breaks — your center of gravity changes as your baby grows

Activity and Relaxation

  • Continue gentle exercises such as walking and stretching
  • Practice breathing and relaxation techniques in preparation for labor
  • Avoid standing or sitting for long periods
  • Attend childbirth education or breastfeeding classes if available

Prenatal Care and Follow-up

  • Attend frequent prenatal visits as recommended by your provider
  • Discuss signs of labor and when to seek medical help
  • Talk about your birth plan with your healthcare team and family

Preparation for Delivery

  • Prepare your hospital bag early
  • Learn the signs of labor
  • Attend childbirth and breastfeeding classes
  • Create a birth plan with your healthcare provider
