Immunization During Pregnancy

Immunization during pregnancy protects both the mother and the baby from serious infections. Vaccines help build immunity that can be passed to the baby before birth.

Importance of Immunization

  • Prevents life-threatening infections
  • Protects newborns in the first months of life
  • Reduces pregnancy-related complications

Common Recommended Vaccines

  • Tetanus toxoid (TT)
  • Tdap (where available)
  • Other vaccines as advised by your healthcare provider

Always consult your healthcare provider to ensure vaccines are given at the correct time during pregnancy.
