Danger Signs Awareness

During pregnancy, many changes happen in your body as it adapts to support your growing baby. While many symptoms are normal and expected, some signs may indicate serious health problems that require immediate medical attention.

Understanding pregnancy danger signs helps protect both the mother and the baby. Early recognition and prompt medical care can prevent complications and save lives.

Danger signs are symptoms that should never be ignored. Even if you are unsure whether a symptom is serious, it is always safer to consult your midwife, obstetrician, or healthcare provider.

Acting quickly when warning signs appear can reduce the risk of:

  • Severe pregnancy complications
  • Preterm labor or miscarriage
  • Infection or internal bleeding
  • Maternal and fetal distress
  • Long-term health problems for mother or baby
